Fried Eggs

Description

This is a recipe for fried eggs....

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter on griddle over low heat.
  2. Crack eggs onto melted butter.
  3. When eggs are firm enough, flip them over.
  4. Cook until desired doneness. Salt and pepper to taste. Serve on top of toast if desired.
